Flavors of South India

South Indian cuisine is renowned for its rich use of spices, aromatic flavors, and unique textures. The region’s dishes vary from state to state — from Tamil Nadu’s crispy dosas to Kerala’s coconut-based curries and Andhra’s spicy biryanis.
