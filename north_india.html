About North India

North India is known for its majestic mountains, rich heritage, and spiritual legacy. The region is home to the Himalayas, the holy River Ganga, and historic cities like Delhi, Agra, and Varanasi. Famous for iconic landmarks such as the Taj Mahal and the Golden Temple, North India offers a vibrant mix of cultures, languages, and cuisines. It celebrates festivals like Diwali, Holi, and Baisakhi with great enthusiasm, making it a culturally dynamic and spiritually enriching part of India.

Famous Tourist Spots

North India is home to world-renowned tourist spots such as the Taj Mahal (Agra), Golden Temple (Amritsar), Qutub Minar (Delhi), Valley of Flowers (Uttarakhand), and Dal Lake (Srinagar).
