Flavors of West India

West Indian cuisine is famous for its rich diversity, bold spices, and coastal as well as vegetarian specialties. The region includes dishes from Maharashtra, Gujarat, Rajasthan, Goa, and Madhya Pradesh, blending sweet, spicy, tangy, and savory flavors.

Vada Pav

Maharashtra’s iconic street food — a spicy potato fritter sandwiched in a soft bun, served with chutneys.

Dhokla

A steamed savory cake made from fermented chickpea flour, lightly spiced and often garnished with mustard seeds — a Gujarat specialty.
