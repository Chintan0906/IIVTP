About West India

West India is a vibrant region known for its diverse culture, rich history, and stunning landscapes. It includes states like Maharashtra, Gujarat, Rajasthan, Goa, and Madhya Pradesh. From the bustling city of Mumbai to the serene beaches of Goa, West India offers a unique blend of urban and rural experiences. The region is famous for its festivals, cuisine, and historical landmarks such as the Ajanta and Ellora Caves.
