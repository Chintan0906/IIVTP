About East India

East India is a land of breathtaking beauty, cultural richness, and deep-rooted traditions. Stretching from the Bay of Bengal to the hills of the Northeast, it includes states like West Bengal, Odisha, Bihar, and Jharkhand. The region is home to stunning temples, lush forests, and diverse cuisines. From the mesmerizing Sun Temple of Konark to the Sunderbans mangrove delta, East India is a blend of spirituality, history, and natural wonders.
